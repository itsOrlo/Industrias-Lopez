Embark on Unforgettable Customized Tours in Ecuador with Cielo Verde

Are you ready to unlock the beauty and diversity of Ecuador like never before? At Cielo Verde, we are your trusted partners in crafting personalized adventures that allow you to explore the very best that this enchanting country has to offer. Join us on a journey that transcends typical travel experiences and immerses you in the heart of Ecuador's culture, nature, and language.

Ecuador: Where Learning Spanish Meets Adventure

At Cielo Verde, we understand the allure of combining language learning with immersive travel experiences. Our programs are designed to create a unique synergy between linguistic exploration and the discovery of Ecuador's wonders. Picture this: as you study and learn Spanish, you embark on a captivating journey through the heart of Ecuador.

Journey Through Ecuador: Learn Spanish, Explore Cities, and Immerse in Culture

Our flagship program, "Journey Through Ecuador," is your passport to a transformative experience. As you traverse the country, from bustling cities to quaint towns, and from one picturesque location to another, you'll find yourself immersed in a world of language and culture.


Spanish Learning: Throughout your journey, you'll engage in 20 hours of Spanish classes per week. Our dedicated instructors will guide you through the intricacies of the Spanish language, ensuring that you not only learn but also communicate effectively in real-world settings. Exploration: Every step of your adventure is carefully curated to offer a perfect blend of language acquisition and cultural immersion. You'll have the opportunity to explore cities, indigenous villages, serene lagoons, and awe-inspiring volcanoes. Activities such as swimming, hiking, horseback riding, and community visits will enrich your experience, providing a holistic perspective of Ecuador's diverse landscapes. The Sierra and the Oriente: Our unique program seamlessly combines the enchanting highlands (sierra) with the lush wonders of the Oriente or Amazon. It's an expedition that takes you from the Andean peaks to the verdant rainforests, ensuring you witness the ecological and cultural tapestry of Ecuador.

About us

About Us

Cielo Verde, a young company passionate about showing the beauty of Ecuador through unique travel experiences. Founded in 2023, we have experienced guides who share a deep love for our country and a wealth of knowledge about local tourist destinations. Our mission is to offer a total immersion in Ecuadorian culture and language while exploring the natural and cultural wonders of Ecuador.

What We Do

We offer more than just tourist trips. We design authentic, personalized experiences that allow you to explore fascinating cities or create your own itinerary. In addition to exploring Ecuador's most iconic destinations, our trips include the opportunity to learn and perfect your Spanish at a renowned Spanish school. You will immerse yourself in local culture, history, and cuisine, creating unforgettable memories while gaining fluency in Spanish.

Our Commitment

At Cielo Verde, we are committed to providing you with a safe, enriching, and authentic travel experience. Our experienced guides will accompany you every step of the way, sharing their knowledge and passion for Ecuador. We strive to provide high-quality service, fostering respect for nature and local communities we visit. Furthermore, our commitment to Spanish language instruction will allow you to dive even deeper into local culture.

Mission and Vision

Mission

We want to offer and provide a valuable service differentiated and personalized to each of our clients, in each of our tourist packages that are unique and Affordable prices managing to exceed each of the expectations of our most demanding customers, such as the German market in general the european market and why not the entire world. Also driven by the constant interest in continuous improvement, through our dedication and effort in our daily work. with a vocation for service and innovation. To more quality, reliability and honesty and good service

Vision

Our vision is to establish a tourism company that not only shows natural landscapes and tourist attractions, but is also committed to teaching and disseminating the Spanish language. We aspire to share and discover customs, traditions, and cultures that enrich the spirit of those who visit us. We achieve this goal through spiritual experiences, such as visiting a shaman. We strive to be different from many other tourism companies that are limited to pleasing the eye; our purpose is to provide an experience that not only satisfies the sense of sight, but also the soul and spirit of each person who chooses us
